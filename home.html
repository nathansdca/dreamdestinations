Europe

Europe
From the thrill of fresh powder on the mountain top to the peace of a twilight stroll through a gorgeous medieval town, Europe is a place that stimulates your sense of curiosity. Through shared history, cultural diversity, and natural beauty, this continent inspires connections, experiences and memories that stretch beyond borders. In a continent where travel is free-flowing, adventures are limitless. (https://visiteurope.com/en/i-am-curious/)
Asia
This vast continent, with more than half the world’s population, has ancient temples, striking natural beauty and some of the world’s largest cities. In Asia, visit peaceful countryside, serene temples, glorious beaches and ultra-modern cities. From the nomadic steppes of Kazakhstan to the frenetic streets of Hanoi, Asia is a continent so full of intrigue, adventure, solace and spirituality that it has fixated and confounded travellers for centuries. (https://www.lonelyplanet.com/asia, https://www.expedia.com/Asia)
Africa
Africa abounds with incredible sights, from soaring sand dunes to savannas teeming with wildlife. From outdoor adventures in national parks to exotic cuisine and an incredible diversity in cultures, Africa is arguably the world’s most underrated continent. (https://www.travel-experience-live.com/why-travel-to-africa, https://www.nationalgeographic.com/ expeditions/destinations/africa)
